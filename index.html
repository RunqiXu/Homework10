Net overseas migration by country of birth in Australia before and after travel restriction

Net overseas migration shows people from different countries who move in to or move out from Austrila.
In recent years, overseas migration in Austrila shows a significant increase trend. In this visualisation, we focus on the net overseas migration in Austrila in 2019 and 2021.

This visualisation is created by Runqi Xu. The datasource is Net overseas migration by country of birth in Australia.
